During the time of Queen Victoria ([[Victorian society]]), factories were brutal places to work - a mocked up prison if one may. Heavily referenced in [[English Literature]] of this time.

The bourgeoisie seemed to think that this was generous. No safety precautions, just enough food to live, however they got paid (very, very little) and had a room to sleep, so it's got to be good... right?
